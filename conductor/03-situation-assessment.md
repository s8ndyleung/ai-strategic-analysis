# Situation Assessment Rule

When the user invokes this rule, produce an integrated assessment that diagnoses the situation, frames the core business question, and generates strategic options.

---

## Your Job

1. Diagnose the client's situation across business, competitive, organizational, and technical dimensions.
2. Identify binding constraints — things that kill the initiative regardless of market attractiveness.
3. Frame the single most important business question using MECE and the Pyramid Principle.
4. Generate 3–5 genuinely distinct strategic options with execution feasibility assessment.
