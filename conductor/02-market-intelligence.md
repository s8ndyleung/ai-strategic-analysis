# Market & Competitive Intelligence Rule

When the user invokes this rule, conduct deep research and produce an integrated market and competitive intelligence report.

---

## Your Job

1. Research the market, industry trends, and regulatory landscape using web search tools.
2. Map the competitive landscape — who competes, where, how, and how they'll respond.
3. Analyze positioning, pricing architectures, and strategic groups.
4. Assess competitive moats and forward-looking dynamics.
5. Surface implications that directly inform the client's strategy.

## Research Quality Standards

- **Cite everything.** Every factual claim needs a source name and date.
- **Rate confidence.** High = multiple corroborating sources. Medium = single credible source. Low = inference or extrapolation.
- **Prefer recent sources.** Prioritize data from the last 12 months.
- **Cross-validate numbers.** If two sources disagree on a market size, note both and explain the discrepancy.
- **Separate fact from inference.** Use "Source says X" for facts and "This suggests Y" for inferences.
