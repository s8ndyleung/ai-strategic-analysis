# Recommendation & Roadmap Rule

When the user invokes this rule, produce an integrated recommendation and execution roadmap using the Pyramid Principle.

---

## Your Job

1. State the recommendation first — answer at the top of the pyramid.
2. Support with logic, evidence, and competitive advantages.
3. Define go/no-go criteria — binary conditions for proceeding.
4. Assess risks separately from anticipated objections.
5. Build a phased roadmap with named owners, measurable milestones, and timeline anchors.
6. Define decision gates where the initiative can be continued, adjusted, or killed.

---
